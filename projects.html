BearWatch
There when you aren't.

Protect your stuff! If you value your possessions, BearWatch is the perfect safeguard! BearWatch is a tower that watches over your belongings when you aren't there to keep an eye on it yourself. This tower sports two cameras for maximum security. The bottom camera sounds an alarm when your stuff goes missing, and the top camera tracks and records the sneaky thief.
